What is an AIRM Supplement?

The AIRM is the reference vocabulary covering global ATM concepts. Regional concepts are supported as Supplements.

Available Supplements

European Supplement

The European Supplement elaborates the main content of the AIRM by bringing in terms and definitions from European Union regualtions and SESAR deliverables. Furthermore, it contains:

  • the AIRM Constraints Model that provides the constraints thathave an impact on the AIRM Conceptual Model and/or the AIRM Logical Model.
  • the Environment subject dealing with gaseous and noise emissions.
