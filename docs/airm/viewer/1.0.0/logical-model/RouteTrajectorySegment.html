RouteTrajectorySegment

urn:aero:airm:1.0.0:LogicalModel:Subjects:Flight:Movement:RouteTrajectorySegment

A portion of the flight route or trajectory.

Source: ICAO Information Management Panel (IMP)
Synonyms: RouteTrajectoryElement
Parent concept: GeoTemporalEnabledEntity

Properties:

Properties Definition Type
assignedSpeed The True Airspeed for the first or the whole cruising portion of the flight. ValSpeedType
procedure The procedure used by the trajectory element. Procedure
route The route used by the trajectory element. Route
routePortion The route portion used by the trajectory element. RoutePortion
routeSegment The published route segment on which the trajectory segment is based. RouteSegment
taxiInClearance The clearance giving the cleared taxi path. TaxiInClearance
alternateAerodrome Alternate aerodrome applicable to this trajectory element. Aerodrome
startPoint The starting point of the trajectory segment. RouteTrajectoryPoint
endPoint The ending point of the route trajectory segment. RouteTrajectoryPoint
specialRequirements Applicable special requirements to this trajectory element. This is an indicator that the flight is expected to be operating in accordance with regulations issued by the relevant State for aircraft operating as State aircraft, as per Article 3 of the Convention on International Civil Aviation (Doc 7300) and for aircraft operating in accordance with State regulations for non-standard flying activities, normally through the use of reserved airspace.

CharacterString

rateOfClimbDescent Average rate of climb and descent for the leg (ft per minute), derived from rateOfAltitudeChange of the two Waypoints delimiting the FlightSegment.

Integer

mainEnginesFuelBurn The amount of fuel burn of the main engines during the flight segment.

Decimal

type The type of TrajectorySegment. CodeRouteTrajectorySegmentType
fuelBurnRate Average Fuel burn rate for the leg. Derived from the deltaFuelBurn for the WayPoints delimiting the FlightSegment.

Number

fuelBurn The fuelBurn for the FlightSegment, expressed in kg.

Number

flightRules The regulation, or combination of regulations, that governs all aspects of operations under which the pilot plans to fly that trajectory element. CodeFlightRulesType
exceedLength A value used to indicate that only Direct Flight Segments (DCT) that exceed this value are concerned by a Flight Restriction. ValDistanceType

TM_Duration

Integer
