CodeInstrumentRunwayType

urn:aero:airm:1.0.0:LogicalModel:Subjects:AirTrafficOperations:Codelists:CodeInstrumentRunwayType

A description of the types of runway intended for the operation of aircraft using instrument approach procedures.

Source: ICAO Annex 1ICAO Annex 14

Properties:

Properties Definition Type
PRECISION_APPROACH_RUNWAY_CATEGORY_I An instrument runway served by ILS and/or MLS and visual aids intended for operations with a decision height not lower than 60 m (200 ft) and either a visibility not less than 800 m or a runway visual range not less than 550 m.

enum value

NON_PRECISION_APROACH_RUNWAY An instrument runway served by visual aids and a non-visual aid providing at least directional guidance adequate for a straight-in approach.

enum value

NON_INSTRUMENT_RUNWAY A runway intended for the operation of aircraft using visual approach procedures.

enum value

MLS_PRECISION_APPROACH_RUNWAY Microwave landing system precision approach runway.

enum value

PRECISION_APPROACH_RUNWAY_CATEGORY_III_D ILS precision approach runway category III D

enum value

PRECISION_APPROACH_RUNWAY_CATEGORY_III_C An instrument runway served by ILS and/or MLS to and along the surface of the runway and intended for operations with no decisions height and no runway visual range limitations.

enum value

PRECISION_APPROACH_RUNWAY_CATEGORY_III_B An instrument runway served by ILS and/or MLS to and along the surface of the runway and intended for operations with a decision height lower than 15 m (50 ft), or no decision height and runway visual range less than 200 m but not less than 50 m.

enum value

PRECISION_APPROACH_RUNWAY_CATEGORY_III_A An instrument runway served by ILS and/or MLS to and along the surface of the runway and intended for operations with a decision height lower than 30 m (100 ft), or no decision height and runway visual range not less than 200 m.

enum value

PRECISION_APPROACH_RUNWAY_CATEGORY_II An instrument runway served by ILS and/or MLS and visual aids intended for operations with a decision height lower than 60 m (200 ft) but not lower than 30 m (100 ft) and a runway visual range not less than 350 m.

enum value
