VerticalStructure

urn:aero:airm:1.0.0:LogicalModel:Subjects:Common:Geospatial:VerticalStructure

All fixed and mobile objects, or parts thereof that extend above the surface of the Earth.

Source: EUROCONTROL-FAA AIXM v.5.1
Parent concept: GeoTemporalEnabledEntity
Children: AerodromeSign; Bridge; Facility; Terminal;

Properties:
