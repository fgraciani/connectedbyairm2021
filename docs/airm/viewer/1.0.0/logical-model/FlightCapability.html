FlightCapability

urn:aero:airm:1.0.0:LogicalModel:Subjects:Flight:FlightCapability

The capabilities of the flight comprising of the: a) presence of relevant serviceable equipment on board the aircraft; b) equipment and capabilities commensurate with flight crew qualifications; and c) where applicable, authorization from the appropriate authority.

Source: ICAO Doc 4444
Parent concept: TemporalEnabledEntity

Properties:

Properties Definition Type
communicationCapability The capability of the aircraft and associated flight crew qualifications to communicate with ATS units. CodeCommunicationCapabilityType
aircraftCapability The ability of an aircraft or an aircraft equipment to provide a service or perform a function that, either on its own or with other services or functions, can deliver a definable level of performance. AircraftCapability
flightCrewApplicationAndApproval Equipment and procedures for which the air crew is qualified. FlightCrewApplicationAndApproval
rnpCapability Indication about which Required Navigation Performance requirements can be met by the aircraft while operating in the context of a particular airspace when supported by the appropriate navigation infrastructure. CodeRNPCapabilityType
rnavCapability Indication about which Area Navigation requirements can be met by the aircraft while operating in the context of a particular airspace when supported by the appropriate navigation infrastructure. CodeRNAVFlightCapabilityType
navigationCapability The navigation capability of the aircraft and associated flight crew qualifications. CodeNavigationCapabilityType
