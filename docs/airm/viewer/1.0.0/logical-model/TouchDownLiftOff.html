TouchDownLiftOff

urn:aero:airm:1.0.0:LogicalModel:Subjects:BaseInfrastructure:AerodromeInfrastructure:TouchDownLiftOff

A load bearing area on which a helicopter may touch down or lift off.

Source: ICAO Annex 1
Synonyms: Touchdown and Lift-off Area
Abbreviations: TLOF
Parent concept: GeoTemporalEnabledEntity

Properties:

Properties Definition Type
designator The designator of the touch down and lift-off area.

CharacterString

helicopterClass The class of a helicopter associated with a touch down and lift-off area. AircraftCategory

contaminant Touch down lift-off contaminant. TouchDownLiftOffContamination
associatedAerodrome The aerodrome the touch down lift-off zone is associated with. Aerodrome
connectingGuidanceLine A GuidanceLine which connects to the TouchDownLiftOff GuidanceLine

approachTakeOffArea The approach and take off runway area containing the touchdown lift off zone. Runway

touchDownLiftOffMarking The Marking marked on the TouchDownLiftOff TouchDownLiftOffMarking
touchDownLiftOffSafeArea A safe area protecting the touch down lift-off area. TouchDownLiftOffSafeArea
navaid Navaid installed at a particular landing area. RadioNavigationService
width The physical width of the touchdown and lift-off area. ValDistanceType
slope The slope (rate of upward inclination of the surface from the horizontal) of the surface of a touch down and lift-off area.
