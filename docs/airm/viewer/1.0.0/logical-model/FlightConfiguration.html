FlightConfiguration

urn:aero:airm:1.0.0:LogicalModel:Subjects:Aircraft:FlightConfiguration

Configuration of aircraft in operation.

Source: SESAR
Parent concept: AircraftConfiguration
Children: TakeOffConfiguration;

Properties:

Properties Definition Type
aircraftMass The mass of the aircraft at a specific time during the flight. ValMassType
mainEnginesFuelFlow The fuel flow for the main engines of an aircraft at a specific time during a flight in terms of amount per time unit.

Decimal

Boolean

Boolean

Boolean

Boolean

Decimal
