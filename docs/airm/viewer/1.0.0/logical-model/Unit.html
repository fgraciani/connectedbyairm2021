Unit

urn:aero:airm:1.0.0:LogicalModel:Subjects:Stakeholders:Stakeholder:Unit

A generic term referring to all types of entities providing all types of ATM related services.

Source: EUROCONTROL-FAA AIXM v.5.1
Parent concept: GeoTemporalEnabledEntity

Properties:

Properties Definition Type
designator A distinguishing label, term, abbreviation or acronym used to identify the Unit.

CharacterString

airspaceUnderResponsibility The Airspace which is under the responsibility of the Unit Airspace
areaOfResponsibility The airspace wherein flights are under the control of the ATSU. Airspace
areaOfInterest The volumetric extension of the area of resposibility of an ATSU that allows detecting flights of interest. Airspace
aerodromeLocation The aerodrome where the unit is located. Aerodrome
surveillanceRadar SurveillanceRadar utilised by the Unit SurveillanceRadar
service The service provided by the Unit Service
contact Contact details for the unit. ContactInformation
ownerOrganisation The organisation to which the unit belongs. Organisation
availability The operational status of the unit. UnitAvailability
relatedUnit The related unit. Unit
unitRole The role played by the unit. CodeRoleKindType
unitResponsibility The responsibility of the unit. CodeResponsibilityKindType
type An indicator of the type of unit, usually related to the standard type of services provided by it. CodeUnitType
name The full textual name of a unit, established according to the rules specified by International Civil Aviation Organisation (ICAO), and specifically in the official language of the country, transposed into the Latin Alphabet where necessary.

CharacterString

military An indicator of the operational nature of the unit. CodeMilitaryOperationsType
locationIndicatorICAO A four-letter code group formulated in accordance with rules prescribed by ICAO and assigned to the ATS unit.

CharacterString

isIOPEnabled Indication whether the unit is IOP-enabled.

Boolean

isCpdlcEnabled Indication whether CPDLC facility is enabled.

Boolean

isCompliantICAO An indicator that the Unit is setup according to the International Civil Aviation Organisation (ICAO) Standards and Recommended Practices (SARPS).

Boolean

isAirspaceManagementCell Indicates whether the Unit is an AMC, a joint civil/military unit responsible for the day-to-day management and temporary allocation of national or sub-regional airspace under the jurisdiction of one or more ECAC state(s).

Boolean
