SeaplaneLandingArea

urn:aero:airm:1.0.0:LogicalModel:Subjects:BaseInfrastructure:AerodromeInfrastructure:SeaplaneLandingArea

A defined area on water at an aerodrome prescribed for the landing and take-off of seaplanes.

Source: ICAO
Synonyms: Seaplane Run
Abbreviations: SLA
Parent concept: GeoTemporalEnabledEntity

Properties:

Properties Definition Type
coordinatedUse The primary coordinated use of the waterway. CodeWaterOperatingAreaCoordinatedUseType
associatedAerodrome The aerodrome the seaplane landing area is associated with. Aerodrome

dockingArea A docking area used by the seaplane landing area. DockingArea
dockSite Seaplane landing area dock site. FloatingDockSite
availability The operational status of the seaplane landing area. ManoeuvringAreaAvailability
markingBuoy A buoy used to mark the SeaplaneLandingArea MarkingBuoy
taxiChannel A taxi channel used at the seaplane landing area. TaxiChannel
turningBasin A turning basin used at the seaplane landing area. TurningBasin
waterLaneEnd A water lane end used for take-off and landing. WaterLaneEnd
rampSite Landing area ramp site location. SeaplaneRampSite
width The overall width measurement of the seaplane landing area to the nearest 5 feet. ValDistanceType
tidalRange The calculated height difference between the mean low tide and the mean high tide. ValDistanceType
name Name of the feature water body (river/lake).

CharacterString

length The overall length measurement of the seaplane landing area to the nearest 5 feet. ValDistanceType
currentFlowRate The measured current flow rate in the Water Operating Area. ValSpeedType
currentFlowCardinalDirection Code indicating the cardinal compass location of the turning basin from centroid of the water lane end. CodeCompassPointType
coordinatedUseActivityLevel The amount of activity based on percentage of daily use of the primary coordinated use type. If coordinated use type is multiple provide the largest activity level of the single most expected activity.

Integer
