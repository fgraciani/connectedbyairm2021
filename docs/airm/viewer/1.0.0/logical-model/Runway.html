Runway

urn:aero:airm:1.0.0:LogicalModel:Subjects:BaseInfrastructure:AerodromeInfrastructure:Runway

A defined rectangular area on a land aerodrome prepared for the landing and take-off of aircraft.

Source: ICAOICAO Annex 1ICAO Annex 14
Parent concept: TemporalEnabledEntity
