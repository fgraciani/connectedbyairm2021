ArrivalManagementAdvisory European Supplement

urn:aero:ses:eurocontrol:airm:1.0.0:LogicalModel:Subjects:AirTrafficOperations:TrafficSynchronization:ArrivalManagement:ArrivalManagementAdvisory

An arrival management advisory to a flight to achieve its landing at the AMAN Planned Threshold Time (APTT).

Source: SESAR
Supplements: Entity

Properties:

Properties Definition Type
speedAdvisoryReferenceType The reference type of the speed advised. CodeSpeedReferenceType
advisedHolding The holding advised to synchronize traffic. HoldingProcedure
advisedProcedure The procedure advised to synchronize traffic. Procedure
advisedTransition The procedure transition advised to synchronize traffic. ProcedureTransition
advisedRoute The route advised to synchronize traffic. Route
advisedSTAR The STAR advised to synchronize traffic. StandardInstrumentArrival
arrival The arrival (i.e. flight) the advisory is valid for. ArrivalOperations
currentSequence The sequence the advisory is designed to implement. LandingSequence
timeToLoseOrGainOnTheGround Ground Delay proposed to be applied at the departure aerodrome in order to land at the AMAN Planned Threshold Time (APTT).

TM_Duration

timeToGainOrLose An arrival management advisory in the form of the amount of time that a flight is supposed to lose or gain to arrive at the metering fix to land at the AMAN Planned Threshold Time (APTT).

TM_Duration

speedAdvisoryValue The value of the speed advised. ValSpeedType
