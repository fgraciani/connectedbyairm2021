TaxiRoute European Supplement

urn:aero:ses:eurocontrol:airm:1.0.0:LogicalModel:Subjects:BaseInfrastructure:AerodromeInfrastructure:TaxiRoute

A predefined path from a Runway to an aircraft Stand (and vice versa). A taxi route is a sequence of taxiways.

Source: FOIPS
Supplements: TemporalEnabledEntity

Properties:

Properties Definition Type
intendedPosition The intended position at the end of the taxi route.

CharacterString

guidanceLine A guidance line on the taxi route. GuidanceLine
taxiway A taxiway composing the taxi route. Taxiway
runwayConfiguration The runway configuration for which the taxi route is established. RunwayConfiguration
runwayDirection A runway direction served by the taxi route. RunwayDirection
taxiwayConfiguration The taxiway configuration for which the taxi route is established. TaxiwayConfiguration
type The type of movement (Taxi-in or Taxi-out) allowed along the TaxiRoute. A TaxiRoute connects a Stand and a Runway either to go IN the Runway or to go OUT from the Runway. CodeTypeOfTaxiRouteType
trafficSituation The traffic situation.

CharacterString

taxiTime The taxi time for the taxi route.

TM_Duration

taxiRouteProcedure The procedures applicable to the taxi route.

CharacterString

taxiRouteConstraint A limitation applied to the taxi route.

CharacterString

status Indication whether the taxi route is the planned one or the cleared one, which may be a deviation of it. CodeTaxiRouteStatusType
startPosition The start of the taxi route.

CharacterString

mode The taxi route mode. CodeTaxiRouteModeType
isAllowed Indication whether the usage of the taxi route is allowed or prohibited, depending on runway configuration.

Boolean
