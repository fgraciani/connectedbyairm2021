MCDMCoordination European Supplement

urn:aero:ses:eurocontrol:airm:1.0.0:LogicalModel:Subjects:AirTrafficOperations:DemandAndCapacityBalancing:MCDMCoordination

Coordination, in the context of Measure Collaborative Decision Making, for an ATFM measure or an action on a flight.

Source: SESAR
Supplements: TemporalEnabledEntity

Properties:

Properties Definition Type
coordinationLevel The level at which the coordination is being done. CodeMCDMCoordinationLevelType
sourceHotspot ATFM hotspot at the origin of the MCDM coordination. ATFMHotspot
coordinatedMeasure ATFM measure in the context of which the coordination is done. ATFMMeasure
involvedUserCategory A category of users involved in a coordination for an ATFM measure. MCDMCoordinationUserCategoryInvolvement
initiator The initiator of the MCDM coordination Unit
actor A role that a user plays within the MCDM topic. MCDMCoordinationActor
actedUponFlight Flight being acted upon by the MCDM coordination at flight level. Flight
actionOnFlight Coordination at the level of a flight within the coordination at measure level. MCDMCoordination
timeToStartImplementation The deadline to start the implementation activities.

DateTime

timeToImplement The deadline to finish the implementation activities.

DateTime

timeToCoordinate The deadline to finish the coordination activities.

DateTime

isInitiatorImplementer Indication whether the initiator of the coordination is the implementer.

Boolean

hasLocalImpactOnly Indication whether the MCDMCoordination has local impact only and therefore involves only adjacent (upstream/downstream) FMPs instead of all FMPs along the flight path.

Boolean

coordinationStatus The status of the MCDM coordination. CodeMCDMCoordinationStatusType
