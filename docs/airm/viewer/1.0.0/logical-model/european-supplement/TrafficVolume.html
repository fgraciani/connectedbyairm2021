TrafficVolume European Supplement

urn:aero:ses:eurocontrol:airm:1.0.0:LogicalModel:Subjects:AirTrafficOperations:DemandAndCapacityBalancing:TrafficVolume

A set of conditions used to identify the flights over an airspace, point, aerodrome or set of aerodromes so that they can be monitored or regulated within the tactical/pre-tactical Air Traffic Flow and Capacity Management (ATFCM) system.

Source: EUROCONTROL ATM Lexicon
Abbreviations: TFV
Supplements: TemporalEnabledEntity

Properties:

Properties Definition Type
designator Designator of the traffic volume as defined by the Network Manager.

CharacterString

hotspot ATFM hotspot related to the traffic volume. ATFMHotspot
capacity The capacity of the traffic volume. Capacity
otmv The occupancy traffic monitoring value of the traffic volume. OccupancyTrafficMonitoringValue
referenceLocation Location where the traffic volume is situated. ReferenceLocation
filteredFlight Flight that satisfies the criteria of the traffic volume. Flight
activation The times during which the traffic volume is active. TrafficVolumeActivation
tfvSet Traffic volume set to which the traffic volume belongs. TrafficVolumeSet
linkedFlow A flow which is lined to the traffic volume. Flow
isMonitorable Indicates whether the Traffic Volume shall be monitored (i.e. when the sector is active in a sector configuration).

Boolean
