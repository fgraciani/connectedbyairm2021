WeatherCondition

urn:aero:airm:1.0.0:LogicalModel:Subjects:Meteorology:WeatherCondition

Weather observations or forecast for an area of interest.

Source: EUROCONTROL-FAA WXXM v.1.1
Parent concept: TemporalEnabledEntity
Children: AviationCondition; WaterBodyCondition;

Properties:

Properties Definition Type
analysisTime The time at which the forecast or observation process starts.

DateTime

contour A contour used to outline a particular weather situation within the forecast or observation. Contour
weatherInformationSource The weather information source. WeatherInformationSource
phenomenon The phenomenon of relevance for the weather condition. WeatherPhenomenon
type The type of weather condition being reported. CodeWeatherConditionType
probability Relative likelihood of a forecast expressed as a percentage.

Decimal

hasNoSignificantWeather Indication that no specified en-route weather phenomena which may affect the safety of aircraft operations is occurring or expected to occur.

Boolean

confidence Quality of trusting for a forecast expressed as a percentage.

Decimal

changeIndicator Indication about the type of meteorological conditions changes. CodeChangeIndicatorType
