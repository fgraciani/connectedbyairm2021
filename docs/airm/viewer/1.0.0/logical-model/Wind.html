Wind

urn:aero:airm:1.0.0:LogicalModel:Subjects:Meteorology:Wind

Air motion relative to the Earth's surface. Unless otherwise specified, only the horizontal component is considered.

Source: WMO International Meteorological Vocabulary
Parent concept: WeatherPhenomenon

Properties:

Properties Definition Type
crosswind A wind blowing perpendicularly to one's direction of travel or orientation. ValSpeedType
windSpeed The speed of the wind. ValSpeedType
windGust Variation from the mean surface wind speed as defined by ICAO Annex 3. ValSpeedType
windDirection Direction from which wind is blowing.

Angle

verticalWindGust Component wind gust in the upward or downward direction. ValSpeedType
minWindSpeed The minimum speed of the wind. ValSpeedType
maxWindSpeed The maximum speed of the wind. ValSpeedType
isVariable Indication that the direction of the wind changes frequently. According to Annex 3 the wind is reported as variable (VRB) in local routine or special reports, in METAR and SPECI, when one of the following situations occur: - when the total variation is 60 or more and less than 180 and the wind speed is less than 1.5 m/s (3 kt), the wind direction shall be reported as variable with no mean wind direction; or - when the total variation is 180 or more, the wind direction shall be reported as variable with no mean wind direction.

Boolean

isCalm Indication that the wind is forecast to be less than 0.5 m/s (1 kt).

Boolean

headwind A wind blowing from directly in front, opposing forward motion or orientation. ValSpeedType
extremeCounterClockwiseWindDirection The extreme counter-clockwise direction from which the wind is blowing, inclusive.

Angle

extremeClockwiseWindDirection The extreme clockwise direction from which the wind is blowing, inclusive.
