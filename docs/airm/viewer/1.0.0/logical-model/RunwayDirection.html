RunwayDirection

urn:aero:airm:1.0.0:LogicalModel:Subjects:BaseInfrastructure:AerodromeInfrastructure:RunwayDirection

One of the two possible landing and/or take-off directions of a runway or Final Approach and Take-off Area (FATO).

Source: DGIWG
Parent concept: TemporalEnabledEntity
