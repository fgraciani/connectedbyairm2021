What is the Community Area?

The AIRM Community Area is a collaboration portal where the members can monitor and contribute to the evolution of the AIRM.

Anyone can join the AIRM community. Use your Eurocontrol OneSky account to log in or request an account if you don't have one yet.

Access*

*On your first log in you will be asked to request access. Requests are resolved in 1-2 working days.
