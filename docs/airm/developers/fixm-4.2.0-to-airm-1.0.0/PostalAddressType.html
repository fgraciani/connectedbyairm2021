PostalAddressType

fb:PostalAddressType

Physical address at which the organization or individual may be contacted. Derived from ISO19115-2003

Properties Definition Type
administrativeArea The state or province of the location or organisation. fb:TextNameType
city The city of the location or organisation. fb:TextCityType
countryCode The country of the physical address for the location or organisation. ISO 3166 abbreviations. fb:TextCountryCodeType
countryName The country of the physical address for the location or organisation. Full name, not ISO 3166 abbreviations. fb:TextCountryNameType
deliveryPoint The street address line for the location. More than one address line may be used. [FIXM] fb:TextAddressType
extension An extension hook for attaching extension (non-core) classes. fb:PostalAddressExtensionType
postalCode The ZIP or other postal code for the location or organisation. fb:TextNameType
