SurfaceContamination

SurfaceContamination

Presence or removal of hazardous conditions on aerodrome/heliport movement areas due to snow, ice, slush, water.

Properties Definition Type
annotation -
criticalRidge A ridge or bank of contaminant that might affect operations. For example a snowbank. -
depth The depth of the contamination on the surface. (decimal) ValDepthType
frictionCoefficient The average ratio of the weight of an object being moved along an airport/heliport surface with contamination and the force that maintains contact between the object and that surface. (decimal) ValFrictionType
frictionDevice The type of equipment used to determine the friction coefficient of an airport/heliport surface with contamination. (string) CodeFrictionDeviceType
frictionEstimation A qualitative estimate of the friction on an airport/heliport surface with contamination. (string) CodeFrictionEstimateType
furtherClearance(string) Time The time when it is expected to complete further clearance of an airport/heliport surface with contamination. (string) TimeType
furtherTotalClearance Indicates that further total clearance of an airport/heliport surface with contamination is expected. (string) CodeYesNoType
layer A layer of contaminant. -
nextObservation(string) Time The date and time of surface contamination measurement completion. (dateTime) DateTimeType
obscuredLights Indicates that the surface lights are obscured by surface contamination. (string) CodeYesNoType
observation(string) Time The date and time of surface contamination measurement completion. (dateTime) DateTimeType
proportion The percentage of the contaminated area of the overall extent of the surface. (decimal) ValPercentType
