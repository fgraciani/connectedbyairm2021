Localizer

Localizer

A component of an ILS consisting of a VHF transmitter, radiating signals in the direction served by the ILS, to provide a straight line descent path in the vertical plane containing the centre line of the runway.

Properties Definition Type
backCourseUsable A code indicating the usability of the localizer signal in the back course sector. (string) CodeILSBackCourseType
declination The angular difference between True North and the station declination (Magnetic North - as indicated by the station). (decimal) ValMagneticVariationType
frequency The frequency of the localizer. (decimal) ValFrequencyType
magneticBearing The measured angle between the localizer beam and Magnetic North at the localizer antenna. (decimal) ValBearingType
magneticBearingAccuracy Accuracy of the measured angle between the localizer beam and Magnetic North at the localizer antenna. (decimal) ValAngleType
trueBearing The measured angle between the localizer beam and True North at the localizer antenna. Note : The True North is the north point at which the meridian lines meet. (decimal) ValBearingType
trueBearingAccuracy Accuracy of the measured angle between the localizer beam and True North at the localizer antenna. (decimal) ValAngleType
widthCourse The localizer course width, in degrees. (decimal) ValAngleType
widthCourseAccuracy Accuracy of the localizer course width, in degrees. (decimal) ValAngleType
