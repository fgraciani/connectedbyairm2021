Service

Service

The provision of tangible goods, information, instructions, guidance, etc. to pilots, flights, aircraft operators and other personnel and institutions concerned with flight operations.

Properties Definition Type
annotation -
availability The operational status of the Service. -
call-sign In aeronautical radio communication the Unit providing a Service is identified by its CALL SIGN. Depending on the type of Service, a Unit may have more than one CALL SIGN, and with each an associated frequency. -
compliantICAO A code indicating whether the service is compliant with the ICAO standards and recommended practices specified for this type of service. (string) CodeYesNoType
flightOperations The destination (arriving, departing, etc.) of the flights for which the information is provided. (string) CodeFlightDestinationType
groundCommunication The point of contact on the ground for the service. -
location The location from where the service is provided. -
name A free text name by which the service is identified. (string) TextNameType
radioCommunication The radio frequency on which the service is provided. -
rank The order of priority of the service, such as "primary" or "alternate". (string) CodeFacilityRankingType
serviceProvider The Unit that provides the Service. -
