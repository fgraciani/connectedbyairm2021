AerialRefuellingAnchor

AerialRefuellingAnchor

A prescribed pattern, established by air refuelling points, along which air-to-air refuelling of aircraft is performed.

Properties Definition Type

extent The Surface extent of the AerialRefuellingAnchor. -

outboundCourseType The type of outbound course from the facility or waypoint on which anchor is based. (string) CodeCourseType

turnDirection Direction of the turn (string) CodeDirectionTurnType
verticalExtent The vertical extent of the AerialRefuellingAnchor. -
