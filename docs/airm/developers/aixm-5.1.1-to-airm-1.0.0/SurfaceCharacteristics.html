SurfaceCharacteristics

SurfaceCharacteristics

An entity grouping together the attributes that model characteristics of an airport surface, such as strength, material, etc.

Properties Definition Type
annotation -
classLCN A value which denotes the peak bearing moment the surface can handle repeatedly without shortening its service life. (decimal) ValLCNType
classPCN The bearing strength of a pavement for unrestricted operations. (decimal) ValPCNType
composition The type of the predominant material of which a surface of the movement area is composed. (string) CodeSurfaceCompositionType
evaluationMethodPCN The method used to rate a runway pavement. (string) CodePCNMethodType
maxTyrePressurePCN The maximum allowable tire pressure category related to the Pavement Classification Number (PCN), as a category. (string) CodePCNTyrePressureType
pavementSubgradePCN An indication of the pavement subgrade strength related to the Pavement Classification Number (PCN). (string) CodePCNSubgradeType
pavementTypePCN The pavement behaviour (rigid or flexible) used for determining the Pavement Classification Number (PCN). (string) CodePCNPavementType
preparation The preparation technique(s) applied to a surface composition. (string) CodeSurfacePreparationType
surfaceCondition The quality of the surface of an airport surface. (string) CodeSurfaceConditionType
tyrePressureSIWL One of the characteristics of a surface that represents the maximum aircraft tire pressure that a movement area surface can support. (decimal) ValPressureType
weightAUW One of the characteristics of a surface that represents the maximum total value of the weight of an aircraft that a movement area surface may support, regardless of the landing gear configuration of the aircraft. (decimal) ValWeightType
weightSIWL One of the characteristics of a surface that represents the maximum calculated load on each tire of a landing gear assembly that a movement area surface can support. (decimal) ValWeightType
