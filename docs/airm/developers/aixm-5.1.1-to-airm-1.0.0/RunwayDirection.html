RunwayDirection

RunwayDirection

One of the two landing and take-off directions of a runway for which attributes like Take-off Run Available (TORA), Take-off Distance Available (TODA), Landing Distance Available (LDA), etc. may be defined.

Properties Definition Type
annotation -
approachMarkingCondition The quality of the runway direction marking such as good, fair, poor and other. (string) CodeMarkingConditionType
approachMarkingType The type of runway direction marking related to landing categories such as precision, non-precision and basic. (string) CodeRunwayMarkingType
availability The operational status of the RunwayDirection. -
classLightingJAR A classification of the approach lighting system for a runway direction using as criteria Joint Aviation Requirements JAR-OPS 1 - Subpart E, Appendix 1 to 1.430. (string) CodeLightingJARType
designator A designator for one of the two possible landing and/or take-off directions of a runway or Final Approach and Take-off Area (FATO). (string) TextDesignatorType
elevationTDZ The highest elevation of the runway Touch Down Zone (TDZ) for a runway direction. (string) ValDistanceVerticalType
elevationTDZAccuracy The accuracy of the reported Touch Down Zone elevation of runway direction. (decimal) ValDistanceType
magneticBearing The measured angle between the runway direction and Magnetic North at a given position. (decimal) ValBearingType
patternVFR The direction, i.e. left or right, of the Visual Flight Rules (VFR) flight pattern at an aerodrome/heliport for a runway direction. (string) CodeDirectionTurnType
precisionApproachGuidance Degree to which navigation aids provide accurate approach guidance for a runway direction. (string) CodeApproachGuidanceType
slopeTDZ Touchdown zone longitudinal slope (slope of 1/3 of the runway length from threshold or first 3000 feet for runways longer than 9000 feet) for a runway direction. (decimal) ValSlopeType
startingElement The RunwayElement that starts the RunwayDirection. -
trueBearing The measured angle between the runway direction and True North at a given position. (decimal) ValBearingType
trueBearingAccuracy The accuracy of the measured angle between the runway direction and True North at a given position. (decimal) ValAngleType
usedRunway The runway used by the RunwayDirection. -
