ProcedureTransition

ProcedureTransition

A group of consecutive segments that are part of a branch on an approach procedure, SID or STAR.

Properties Definition Type
annotation -
departureRunwayTransition The group of takeoff areas that may be referenced from the departure. -
instruction Operational instructions that must be observed when flying the ProcedureTransition part. (string) TextInstructionType
trajectory The graphical represetation of the transition. -
transitionId Used strictly for ARINC coding. The identifier will be the initial point for each transition. A transition includes multiple segment legs. (string) CodeDesignatedPointDesignatorType
transitionLeg One segment of a transition. -
type The type of transition. (string) CodeProcedurePhaseType
vectorHeading A heading provided for use, when a departure is designed to incorporate radar vectors at the termination point, in the event ATC instructions are not received prior to or at the termination fix. (decimal) ValBearingType
