NavigationSystemCheckpoint

NavigationSystemCheckpoint

A point established and marked on the surface of an aerodrome allowing the checking of a navigation system (like VOR, GNSS, etc.) or initialisation of an inertial navigation system.

NavigationSystemCheckpoint

A point established and marked on the surface of an aerodrome allowing the checking of a navigation system (like VOR, GNSS, etc.) or initialisation of an inertial navigation system.

Semantic Correspondence
AIRM Concept Definition
InfrastructurePoint A point used in describing the base or airspace infrastructure.
CodeInfrastructurePointUsageType.VOR_CHECK_POINT A point established in support of VOR tests for determining the accuracy of a VOR receiver on the ground or airborne.
CodeInfrastructurePointUsageType.INERTIAL_NAVIGATION_SYSTEM_CHECK_POINT A point established to test the Inertial Navigation System in the aircraft.
