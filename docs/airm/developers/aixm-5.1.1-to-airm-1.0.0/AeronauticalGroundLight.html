AeronauticalGroundLight

A light specifically provided as an aid to air navigation, with the exception of obstacle lights and lights forming part of surface or approach lighting systems

Properties Definition Type
aerodromeBeacon The airport/heliport in the area of which the light is situated. -
annotation -
colour The primary colour of the light source. (string) CodeColourType
flashing An indication whether the light is steady or flashing. (string) CodeYesNoType
location The location of the ground light. -
name The name by which the ground light is known (string) TextNameType
structureBeacon The vertical structure (such as airport tower) identified by the ground light. -
type A classification of the light based on the kind of item that it visually identifies (string) CodeGroundLightingType
